Basic Rules

Possible actions you may take in combat:
Attack, cast a spell, double move, investigate, ready an attack, pass.

DR: Defense rating. Your race’s base defense plus any special abilities and your agility and constitution modifiers

SP: Spell power. Your class’ base spell power plus your intelligence modifier. Determines what enemies have to roll to defend
against certain spells.

CW: Carry weight. Your class’ carry weight multiplier times your strength stat.

SS: Spell slots. How many spells you can cast aside from cantrops.

Modifiers are -2 at 0 and increase by +1 for every two levels in them.
